How to use
SearchType to filter mods by name across all categories.
Category pillsClick a pill to focus one category; click All to reset.
Class filterLimit mods to your class, or keep All Classes for universal affixes. Note: class assignments are incomplete and may be inaccurate.
Slot filterShow only affixes that can roll on a specific item slot. Covers Barbarian, Druid, Necromancer, Rogue, Sorcerer, Spiritborn. Paladin & Warlock (S13) have no slot data yet.
CheckboxTick any mod to add it to your Roll Target list.
Roll TargetToggle to see only your checked mods. Toggle again to return to full list. Picks are saved between sessions.
Clear PicksRemove all selected mods and reset Roll Target mode.
